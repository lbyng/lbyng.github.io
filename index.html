About Me

Hi! I am an undergraduate student in Computer Engineering and Applied Mathematics at the University of Washington. My research interests include robotics, machine learning, and computer vision.

I currently work as a research collaborator at Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (AI2) with PRIOR Team, where I work closely with PhD Jiafei Duan under the supervision of Prof. Ranjay Krishna. I am also conducting research at UW BioRobotics Laboratory, under the supervision of Prof. Blake Hannaford and mentorship of PhD Haonan Peng.

Research Interests

  • General: Robotics, Machine Learning, Computer Vision
  • Emphasis: Robot Learning, Medical Surgical Robotics, Imitation Learning, Embodied AI, Human-Robot Interaction

A complete teleoperation-to-deployment pipeline for the Unitree Go2 quadruped and D1-T robotic arm. Adapted the ACT and Molmo-Act for grounding in custom mobile manipulation settings.

Teleoperation Framework for UFactory Lite 6 Using Gello
Implemented real-time 6-DOF control for the UFactory Lite 6 arm using the Gello framework, with intuitive manipulation in 3D space. It includes trajectory record and playback modules for reproducible experimentation.

Bimanual Franka Control in MuJoCo Using Gello
